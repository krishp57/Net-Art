Our Connected Realities

Curated By Krish

Curatorial Text

This exhibition explores the world of net art, and specifically dives into works that inhabit the digital space and display a deep connection with our own realities. Each piece selected for this show demonstrates how artists uniquely use the internet to create interesting art, that invokes thought and intrigue into audiences about their own lives. These artworks all challenge traditional notions about art, and reflect the creativity and interconnectedness of the internet. By bringing together a diverse number of net art works, the show intends to display the many ways in which the internet fosters new forms of creativity and a connection to our own lives. The exhibition aims to allow viewers to explore the ways in which digital spaces are connected with our personal realities and how they themselves can express themselves using digital art.

"My boyfriend came back from the war" by Olia Lialina

This artwork tells a unorganized, compelling story about a couple being reunited after a war occurred, being told through interactive links and pages.

"On your wrist is the universe" by Adriana Knouf

This interesting piece of net-art is displayed to users through a smart watch, that used real-time astronomical data to generate poetry on your wrist.

"May amnesia never kiss us on the mouth" by Basel Abbas and Ruanne Abou-Rahme

This piece is comprised of over eighty videos and is displayed through an interactive website, showcasing different artistic performances, such as dances, in response to tragedy or as a way of mourning.
